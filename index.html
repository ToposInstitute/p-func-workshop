Workshop on Polynomial Functors

At the Topos Institute and online via Zoom
March, 2021

Participants will learn background material and hear the latest progress on polynomial functors, monads, and comonads, their algebras and coalgebras, as well as on operads, parametric right adjoints, and cofunctors, with applications to combinatorics and higher categories, type theory and logic, containers, databases, lenses, and dynamical systems.

Talks will be recorded for posterity.

(This may be the first in a series of such workshops.)

Organizers

Participation

Anybody interested in participating is welcome. If you would like to attend, please contact David Spivak or Joachim Kock. A zoom link will be sent out a few days before the workshop.
